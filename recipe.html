Let's Bake

Ingredients

  • 170g Dark Chocolate
  • 225g Butter
  • 6 Eggs
  • 90g Wheat Flour
  • 225g Sugar
  • Some Powdered Sugar

Step 1

Melt the chocolate together with the butter in a pan.

Step 2

Mix the eggs with the sugar.

Step 3

Add the Wheat Flour and mix it again.

Step 4

Add the molten chocolate and mix again.

Step 5

Take your favorite dessert glasses and butter them

Step 6

Fill the chocolate mass into the glasses

Step 7

Now put the filled glasses in the oven and bake them for 10 to 13 minutes at 180Â°. When you take them out the middle should be liquid!

Step 8

Serve the cakes with some cream, ice and powdered sugar.
Enjoy!
